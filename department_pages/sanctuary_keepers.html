As the dedicated team responsible for maintaining the cleanliness and sacredness of our church, we kindly ask for your cooperation in adhering to the following rules to ensure that our church environment remains welcoming, tidy, and conducive to worship:

  • Dispose of Trash Properly ->

    Please make use of designated trash bins located throughout the church premises for the disposal of any waste, including tissues, food wrappers, and beverage containers. Avoid leaving trash in pews, hallways, or other common areas.

  • Respect the Sanctuary ->

    The sanctuary is a sacred space dedicated to worship and prayer. Please refrain from eating or drinking inside the sanctuary, and ensure that all personal belongings are removed from pews after services to allow for proper cleaning.

  • Clean Up After Yourself ->

    If you notice any spills, stains, or messes, please take the initiative to clean them up promptly using the provided cleaning supplies. This helps prevent accidents and maintains a clean and safe environment for everyone.

  • Care for Church Property ->

    Treat church property with respect and care. Avoid damaging or defacing furniture, fixtures, or decorative items, and report any maintenance issues or damages to the appropriate authorities.

  • Keep Restrooms Clean ->

    If you use the restrooms, please ensure that you leave them in a clean and sanitary condition for the next person. Dispose of paper towels, wipe down sinks and countertops, and report any maintenance issues to the sanctuary keepers.

  • Be Mindful of Children ->

    While children are welcome in our church, we ask that parents and guardians supervise their children to prevent them from causing damage or creating messes. Encourage children to treat the church with reverence and respect.

  • Volunteer for Cleaning Duties ->

    Consider volunteering your time to assist the sanctuary keepers in cleaning and maintaining the church facilities. Your help is greatly appreciated and ensures that our church remains a clean and inviting space for all who enter.
