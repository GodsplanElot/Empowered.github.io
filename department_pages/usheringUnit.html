Guidelines for Church Etiquette and Dress Code

Members and Visitors



As church ushers, it is our joy and responsibility to ensure that everyone who enters our church experiences a welcoming and reverent environment for worship. To help maintain order and respect during our services, we kindly ask that you adhere to the following guidelines:

  • 1. Seating Arrangement: ->

    • Please follow the guidance of the ushers when entering the sanctuary and finding a seat. They are here to assist you in finding a suitable place to worship.
    • We ask that you refrain from saving seats for others, as this can cause confusion and inconvenience for fellow worshippers.

  • 2. Dress Code: ->

    As a place of worship, we encourage modesty and reverence in attire. While there is no strict dress code, we ask that both members and visitors dress in a manner that is respectful and appropriate for a church setting.

    Avoid clothing that is overly revealing, tight-fitting, or distracting. Choose attire that reflects modesty, humility, and a reverence for God's presence.

  • 3. Christian Conduct: ->

    Please conduct yourself in a manner that reflects Christian values and principles while in church. This includes showing kindness, respect, and consideration for others at all times.

    Refrain from disruptive behavior, side conversations, or using electronic devices during the service, as these can detract from the worship experience for others.

  • 4. Respect for Others: ->

    Be mindful of those around you and strive to create a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere for all worshippers, regardless of background or appearance.

    Respect personal space and privacy, and avoid behaviors that may infringe upon the worship experience of others, such as loud talking or unnecessary movement.

  • 5. Reverence for God's House: ->

    Remember that the church is a sacred space dedicated to the worship of God. Treat the sanctuary and church property with reverence and respect.

    Refrain from eating, drinking, or engaging in activities that are not conducive to worship while inside the sanctuary.

  • 4. Silence Electronic Devices: ->

    Please silence or turn off all electronic devices, including cell phones, tablets, and smartwatches, before entering the sanctuary.

    Refrain from using electronic devices during the service, as they can be distracting to fellow worshippers and disrupt the worship experience.

  • 5. Respectful Conduct: ->

    Maintain an attitude of reverence and respect throughout the service, refraining from unnecessary talking, whispering, or disruptive behavior.

    Be mindful of your actions and their impact on others, and strive to create an atmosphere of reverence and worship.
