Why Attend?

At the Tabernacle Choir, we believe in the transformative power of music to uplift the soul, inspire the heart, and draw us closer to the presence of God. Through our carefully curated selection of hymns, anthems, and spiritual songs, we seek to create an atmosphere where the Holy Spirit can move freely and touch the lives of all who hear our music.
